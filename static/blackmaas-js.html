Let Lightbringer adorn your heart.

Blackmaas.js is the easiest way to incorporate your love for Azazel into your website comps. All you need to do, apart from accepting the Abbadon into your heart, is follow these steps:

  1. Right-click on this link, and save the file to your project scripts folder: blackmaas.js
  2. Add it into the head of your HTML document.
  3. Add some markup:
    • For ipsum text, add class='satan-ipsum' to any text element
    • For placeholder images, add class='satan-image' to any img tag
  4. Customize the elements with additional data attributes. For the list of data attrbutes, click here
  5. Watch your site look more brutal.
